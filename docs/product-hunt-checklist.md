## Launch Day

### Timing
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Post at**: 12:01 AM PT (3:01 AM ET / 4:01 PM Beijing time)
- This gives full 24 hours for voting

### First Hour
1. Post the product on Product Hunt
2. Add maker's comment immediately
3. Share on Twitter/X with link
4. Share on LinkedIn
5. Post in relevant Discord/Slack communities
6. Send to email subscribers
